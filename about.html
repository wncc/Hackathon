Become a better programmer at Hackathon

Come to Hackathon to pitch ideas, form teams, and make something amazing in 24 hours!

What is Hackathon?

At Hackathon you'lll pitch ideas, form teams around the coolest ones, and then spend 24 hours building something awesome. At the end, you'll present to judges.

Oh, and you'll have a lot of fun along the way. Hackathon is kind of like a party, version 2.0.

  • 2. Form a team
  • 3. Go to optional workshops
  • 4. Build something amazing
